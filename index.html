Time Activity
9:30 AM - 10:00 AM Registration
  • Arrival and registration of students and other attendees.
10:00 AM - 10:30 AM Opening Ceremony
  • Welcome Address by Mr. Azril Ali, Dean of the School of Languages, Literacies, and Translation (SoLLAT), Universiti Sains Malaysia
  • Opening Remarks by Andy, CEO of Teleperformance
10:30 AM - 10:45 AM Event Overview and MoU Signing
  • Presentation of the Teleperformance video.
  • Signing Ceremony for the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU).
  • Photo session with all participants.
10:45 AM - 12:00 PM Talks by Invited Speakers from Teleperformance
  • Michael Costevec, Chief of Staff, Teleperformance, Penang
    Title: Blueprint for Success: Strategies for Building a Thriving Career
  • Marcos Ulip, Regional Talent Engagement Lead, Teleperformance, Penang
    Title: Understanding Company Culture: A Key Factor in Choosing Where to Work
  • Dr. Alla Baksh bin Mohamed Ayub Khan, USM
    Title: The importance of internships for undergraduates
12:00 PM Event Conclusion

12:00 PM - 1:00 PM Informal Interviews and Networking
  • On-site interviews conducted by Teleperformance, providing students the opportunity to engage directly with potential employers and offering immediate employment opportunities.
